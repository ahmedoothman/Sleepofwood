The best sleep possible

Here at Sleep Specialists of the Woodlands we provide all patients with a private room in a comfortable atmosphere. We understand how difficult it can be to sleep in a new place or on a hard, metal hospital bed. This is why each of our rooms is equipped with a queen sized Tempur-Pedic mattress and cable television. Temperature is adjustable for our rooms at the patient’s request and we also provide a very spacious, clean shower for the morning after. Please feel free to come take a tour of our facility prior to scheduling. To schedule a visit please call 281-691-2485. We are located at 6912 FM 1488 Magnolia, TX 77354. We look forward to seeing you.
